Explanations: The examples witih good prediction accuracies have sharper edges between different parts of the chair. The different parts of these chairs are mostly perpendicular to each other.

However, for the bad predictions, the chairs tend to have smooth transitions from one part into another or unusual shapes.

The first chair in the bad examples have legs with unusual shapes and smooth connection with the chair's seating area. The model classified a large number of points in the chairs' legs into as part of the seating area.

The second bad example has part of the chair's seating area leveled with the chair's legs, tricking the model into classifying the extended seating area as another leg.

The third bad example has different parts of the chair intersecting each other, making it difficult for the model to classify points near those intersections.
